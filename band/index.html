Bio








Sofie Mae and Drastic Measures are based out of Oakland, CA and are frequently playing in the venues and streets of San Francisco and Oakland. Playing songs filled with soulful catchy melodies and funky beats, you'll get lost in the music making magic that this band offers the audience. Inspired by musicians from Debarge to Smokey Robinson, they bring elements of jazz, funk and soul into their songs that keep the listener wanting more. This is a band that you won't want to miss out on.
